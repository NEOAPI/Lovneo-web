Rental Collect

Short description of the project.

Full details of the project.

Introducing RENTAL COLLECT, a mobile app designed to streamline rental collection for apartment and hostel owners. Managing rent payments at the end of each month can be challenging and time-consuming. Our app addresses these challenges by offering a range of features to simplify the process:

Key Features:

  • Easy Payment Tracking: Record each money transfer and manually mark payments.
  • Instant Notifications: Receive direct notifications for each transaction, eliminating the need to constantly check bank accounts.
  • User Management: Add new users, delete existing ones, and maintain an organized renter database.
  • Payment History: Access detailed payment histories effortlessly.
  • Integration with RENTAL PAY: Renters can make payments directly through the companion app, RENTAL PAY, ensuring seamless communication and efficiency.
  • Special Tools: Utilize integrated notes and a calculator to jot down important information and calculate rents easily.

Solutions Provided:

  • Streamlined Collection: Simplifies the end-of-month rent collection process, making it easier for owners to track payments.
  • Efficient Management: Enables owners to manage renters and payments effectively without manual hassles.
  • Enhanced Communication: Facilitates better communication between renters and owners through instant notifications and direct payment options.
  • Organized Database: Helps maintain an organized database of renters, payment histories, and important notes.
  • Time-Saving: Saves time by automating and simplifying rental management tasks.

With RENTAL COLLECT, rental management becomes efficient, organized, and hassle-free. Experience the future of rental collection and management with our comprehensive, user-friendly solution.
